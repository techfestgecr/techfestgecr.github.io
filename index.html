About Us

Government Engineering College, Rajkot was founded in the year 2004 with a vision to inculcate technical competence, ethical conduct and innovative thinking so our students can creatively solve the problems faced by the society. Our institute which is approved by the "All India Council For Technical Education"(AICTE) and affiliated to "Gujarat Technological University"(GTU), Ahmedabad supports society for their startup and funding as we are one of the center of "STARTUP INDIA" and "SSIP". Besides this, Our institutution offers bachelor programmes in Electronics and Communications, Instrumentation and Control, Computer, Mechanical, Civil and Automobile. We always welcome our students for initiating new ideas and provide necessary help and support.
