Event Name: Urban Football(Non Technical)
Student Team Leader: Shanover Saiyed (84880 24446)

GTU Point: 9

Team: 5-7, 5 Players, 2 substitutes

Details:

Fees is 50 per participant.

A miniature version of soccer with 5 players on each team, with 2 substitutes. Would play against to score maximum goals within limited time frame. It is better known as futsal.

Rules:
Team scoring maximum goals will win.
All regular football rules will apply except the following:
No Throw-in: that means when the ball is outside the play area, the game is resumed by a pass kick and not by hands as of throw-in.
Penalty kick is taken from center point with no keeper to save, your aim is all you need.
Rolling substitution is allowed, that means a player can clap to other player to come-in and out.
More than 5 players at a time on the field would be penalized by a foul.
The matching criteria would depend on total number of teams registered. It may take form of league if teams are less or knockout if teams are more.

Event Flow:
A fixture of registered teams would be prepared. One against ones. And based on the number of teams the tournament may take shape of a league or knockouts.

Judgement Criteria:
If tournament takes shape of league, points will counted to qualify for Semi-final matches. Points are the goals scored, number of matches won, drawn and lost.
Otherwise, if tournament takes the form of knockouts. The winning team will move forward while the losing team will be out of the tournament.

Rules and regulations may change on or during the tournament and is entirely depended on the organizing team.
